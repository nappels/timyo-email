Great Teams Send Emails With Clear Expectations

We're all drowning in email. The dopamine buzz of a new email notification is addictive and productivity killing. Poorly written emails without clear intent can ruin everyone’s ability to focus on getting meaningful work done.

Our time is sacred and inefficient communication has a real cost. While instant messaging has a greater role than ever among teams, Email is still an important and powerful tool to get things done.

The problem with email is that more is not always better and right away is not always right. Timyo helps teams establish the right response at the right time so if you manage a team or simply work in one there’s no better time to lead by example. Timyo helps everyone use email smarter.

Using Timyo in Teams to Save Time

Timyo adds time-saving features for both senders and recipients, providing teams that use it an immediate boost in efficiency and communication.

Assign Actions

When you send an email using Timyo, you can easily communicate whether you’re expecting the email to result in a Reply, a To-Do or Read only. This additional context eliminates any ambiguity for the recipient.

Assign Time

Actions can also be assigned an expected time for completion so the recipient knows if an immediate response is required or it can wait until a later date, helping everyone on the team better organize their time.

Using Timyo as Recipient

Emails sent between Timyo users are automatically organized by expected action and due date, helping to prioritize all your emails before they even get to your inbox.
